## ğŸ“ Learning from the Competition

### What Duolingo Does Well
- Gamification and engagement
- Habit formation mechanics
- Mobile-first design
- **Lesson**: Make learning addictive, not just effective

### What Babbel Does Well
- Practical, real-world content
- Clear grammar explanations
- Structured progression
- **Lesson**: Focus on what users actually need

### What DW Does Well
- Comprehensive, free content
- Cultural integration
- Native speaker materials
- **Lesson**: Authentic content beats contrived lessons

### What Lingoda Does Well
- Accountability (scheduled classes)
- Live speaking practice
- Professional instruction
- **Lesson**: Human connection matters (but is expensive)

### What Anki Does Well
- Scientific algorithm (FSRS)
- Complete customization
- Active recall method
- **Lesson**: Learning science drives results

### What Aeryn-Deutsch Will Do Best
- Combine all of the above
- Keep everything free
- Innovate with unique features
- **Lesson**: The best app is the sum of all strengths
